Welcome to Bulls and Cows!

Gameplay:

You are required to make a guess by entering a 4-digit number. Feedback on the guess is then provided in the form of "bulls" and "cows".

Bulls and Cows:

A "bull" represents a correct digit guessed in the correct position.
A "cow" represents a correct digit guessed in the wrong position.
For example, if the secret number is "1234" and the guess is "1752": There is 1 "bull" (the digit "1" in the first position). There is 1 "cow" (the digit "2" in the third position).
Win by guessing the entire secret number (4 bulls).
